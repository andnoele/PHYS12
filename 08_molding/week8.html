The first time I tried using the mill the mill went straight down and immediately stopped. I later learned that this is because a part of the mill touched the top of the wax and that is not supposed to happen. To solve this problem, a part of the top mold was cut off in Fusion360. The next time issue I tried using the mill, the process I actually worked but the starting point was to close to the edge of the wax, and part of the mold ended up getting cut off. I switched to a new block of wax and started the program again. Some more issues were caused by the mill being too close to the wax. This was solved by adjusting the height of the mill and giving more space between it and the block of wax.
