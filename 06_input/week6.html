<br> <h1>Electronic Input Devices</h1> <h2>Water Sensor</h2> <p> For my pre-built input sensor, I decided to experiment with a Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor v2.0. I chose this sensor because it will be a part of my plant vending machine. </p> <h3>Method</h3> <p> To find the minimum value, maximum value, and the range in between I used a cup of dirt, the sensor, a syringe, and water. Without adding any water, I measured a base value. Then I added 5 ml of water at a time using the syringe and measured the new value. I continue this process until the values "leveled off". </p> <h3>Data</h3> <ul> <li>Minimum: 189</li> <li>Maximum: 487</li> <li>Range: 298</li> </ul> <h3>Graph</h3> <canvas id="myChart1" style="width:100%;max-width:600px"></canvas> <h3>Water Table</h3> <table> <tr> <th>Added Water (mL)</th> <th>Arduino Output</th> </tr> <tr> <th>0</th> <th>487</th> </tr> <tr> <th>5</th> <th>437</th> </tr> <tr> <th>10</th> <th>375</th> </tr> <tr> <th>15</th> <th>204</th> </tr> <tr> <th>20</th> <th>193</th> </tr> <tr> <th>25</th> <th>190</th> </tr> <tr> <th>30</th> <th>189</th> </tr> </table> <h3>Photos & Videos</h3> <img src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/2dHUOmyz8lSyvFL8R9U6sdL6nRA5tzOlORB15A6Zw_GOZmua6UYbNIqjzBb6VzVpAWS9-sshgkWvzLkI-uPv9I878M4IXJmvoHUO3ESTyoNDdXz2_tBQqQmly8kIpglNxmfGow7g_QBh0IEWG_MIM50" alt="All Componenets"> <h4>All Components</h4> <br></br> <img src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/-j7iErBHVIvSJBdyVLmbBzi1NlifLqgYFjSQuLx7IrPdlITqE-_JSrr_fSg_q4iwNA3-ybj0SSeVKjMelGedqQvEgDqGgs3YY8bG5QwRv2zGJsIuXTYUKxlduWVkVhUaKrp7xtFed5-B5StCFP14Avw" alt="Foam Thickness"> <h4>Foam Thickness</h4> <br></br> <img src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/S9LFz8VnxyMQ93ahiU0OjyGRHxziIntscafSQlhfBOCN2r6VKUGD2bEo5h_Re7EEk7vtfQJRWK83-Q1XpBo8l6ZzyuF8-tSUA3lIg15iLFGNsqiZ1YA5mJxPUi40e1vhnKivUFRFy3fbLSpkESVHRzc" alt="Wiring"> <h4>Wiring</h4> <br></br> <img src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/OLqWedGE4df1ua9hpAogPzWZPdR6TWZxpgkB06vj-dQUz31U1sy6E56XsXU1iC63LMGOl49c-ajXfQn-IU9cXTMC4C83rascnRRpg3s2z8BPvkFzKo1OF24difGdgxPTfg05UeWNQGRGB_Aal7NncoI" alt="Example Measurement"> <h4>Example Measurement</h4> <br></br> <br></br> <h2>Capacitive Sensor</h2> <h3>Method</h3> <p> I created my own capacitive sensor by using two pieces of metallic tape.
